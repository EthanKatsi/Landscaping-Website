ABOUT US

Established in 2023 by Ethan Katsiroubas, EK Landscaping is a premier outdoor services company based in North York. With a passion for transforming outdoor spaces into stunning havens, we specialize in a range of services including weeding, gardening, power washing, painting, staining, and more. As a dedicated self-owned business, we take pride in delivering top-notch results for both residential and commercial properties.
At EK Landscaping, we believe that every outdoor area has the potential to become a work of art. Our mission is to bring Ethan's vision to life, curating outdoor environments that reflect individuality and beauty. Whether you're seeking to revive a lackluster garden, refresh the look of your property with a power wash, or enhance its appeal with a fresh coat of paint, our expert team is here to ensure your vision becomes a reality.
